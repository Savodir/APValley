ShareAway

Are you ever in need of a certain tool, and you don't have it?
Do you ever need your clothes washed very quickly and you don't have a working washing machine?

Your problem is our solution!

Our solution provides a sharing platform for every kind of appliance you need!

Our platform will provide an environmentally friendly solution to everyday problems.
By sharing will you not only help create a more social community that is open for sharing their appliances to people in need, but you will make friends.

Our platform is lowcost since you decide the price people pay for your appliances, and by creating an account, you will receive a small amount of ShareCoins for free!

We use our own currency, ShareCoins, for trading and sharing appliances between customers and providers.
Customers pay a small amount of ShareCoin to the provider as compensation for using their appliances.

these providers can in turn use there ShareCoins to pay for other providers.

Don't have anything to share? don't worry you can still purchase ShareCoins in our in-app shop!
